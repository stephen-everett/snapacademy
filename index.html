Mutual aid group in Pomona CA providing healthy meals to the community for free

We are a collective of people with a mission to reduce hunger in our community. Our goal is provide fresh produce, pantry staples, and grab and go meals for FREE. Our fridges will re-distribute food and supplies to those in need, rather than let it go to waste. To get involved, host a fridge, or learn more, please contact us
